ubl4nk
Hi @Roman , Could you give a brief explanation about the AssociationType and its concept & usage ?
Roman 03/19/2023 11:24
AssociationType defines in which way a subprotocol NFT is associated with the identity NFT. I gave an example for the different types in the last contest, this might for understanding it better:
By choosing the association type, you can control how your subprotocol can/will be used. For instance, let's imagine there is a subprotocol that represents friends (so each NFT of the subprotocol would be a friend). You could in theory register that with the three different types, which would change the meaning of the association: - PRIMARY: You can have exactly one (or zero) friends. Probably not very sensible, but in that case it could represent "My best friend" for instance - ORDERED: You can have many friends and they are ordered. That would be like the top 8 friends on MySpace back in the day - ACTIVE: You can have many friends and they are not ordered. This is the classical social media model that Facebook & co. uses
